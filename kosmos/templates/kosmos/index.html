our story

Makeup, makeup, makeup... We love makeup. It helps us enhance our natural features, enabling us to become artists with our faces as our canvases. It does, however, come at a cost: EXPIRATION (other than the actual money it takes to pay for it of course). Makeup, unfortunately, is not forever. It is actually not safe to use expired makeup, as they can cause infections, similar to how eating that expired yogurt made you feel sick to your stomach.

So it's important to remember when you need to replace your makeup. However, this process can be difficult. Perhaps you got excited to use your makeup and completely forgot to write down when you opened it, so you have no idea when you opened it. Or maybe you decided to test it once, but then forgot about it until months later, and have no clue when that one-time use took place? Maybe you pretend to be responsible, writing down when you've opened your products in a notes app, however, you fail to actually remember to check for these dates.

KOSMOS aims to eliminate this problem. No longer do you have to throw out makeup you aren't entirely sure is expired, nor pretend it is okay to use expired makeup (it's not). KOSMOS, your virtual makeup bag, will actually enable you to not only keep track of the contents of your makeup bag digitalized, but also remind you when these contents are expired (watch out for emails from our lovely bot at noreplykosmos@gmail.com)! KOSMOS aims to make this experience less of a hassle and more convenient, all at the click of a button. With your free KOSMOS account, you can browse our database of over 900 makeup products, and pick and choose the ones you'd like to keep track of in your virtual makeup bag.

Beyond that, KOSMOS actually allows you to curate more than a bag of items you already own, but also COLLECTIONS. With KOSMOS Collections, you will be able to create collections of products, complete with descriptions, and even publicize them for other KOSMOS users. You can browse other users' publicized collections in the Public Collections page, and even send users love with hearts.

Finally, since KOSMOS aims to ease the makeup buying and owning experience, KOSMOS allows you to leave reviews on makeup products. As a third-party, we have no bias towards reviews at all, and will show every side.

So what are you waiting for? Get started with KOSMOS today.

our founder

Hi there! My name is Isabella Enriquez. I'm an undergraduate CS student, and the brain behind KOSMOS.

Over the COVID-19 pandemic, I decided to expand my skill set by taking CS50's web development course. Over the past few months, I have been learning web development and doing many projects, which ends in this, my capstone project. At first, I had no idea what to do for the capstone, as this was the first and only project in the course that was to be our own original ideas. Then I thought about makeup, and how I, for some reason, suck at remembering expiry dates, which is really not good. I created KOSMOS to solve this problem, to automate something that is pretty prominent in my life.

After this summer, I will be continuing my undergraduate studies in computer science. I hope to continue working on my web development skills, and also hope to dive into game development as well. Keep up with me on LinkedIn, GitHub, or my personal website in the links below!
